Design Review & Suggestions

While the current version uses bright colors for section headings, this might feel a bit overwhelming. To improve:

  • Refine the color palette – Use softer, complementary shades for a more professional look.
  • Background and font colors – Replace bright tones with pastel or muted shades for easier reading.
  • Consistency in headings – Instead of multiple colors, apply one uniform heading color and vary font weight/style for distinction.
